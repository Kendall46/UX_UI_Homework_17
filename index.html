Typography

A combination between a Display font (Corben) and a Sans Serif (Roboto). Faces that showcase personality and creative style while remaining professional. They express soft curves that are easy on the eyes as well as providing a friendly and natural rhythm.

Spacing

A 12 count column grid with a margin of 20. Using multiples of 4 to define demensions, padding and margins. 150% leading between lines of text.
